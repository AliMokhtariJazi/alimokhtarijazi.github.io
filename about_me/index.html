Bio

During my academic journey, I completed a Bachelor's degree in Electronic Engineering, where I gained expertise in electronics and circuit theory. My dissertation focused on eliminating engineering loss in photonic crystal waveguides, showcasing my ability to tackle complex engineering challenges. However, my fascination with the fundamental nature of the universe led me to pursue a Master's degree in Theoretical Particle Physics. With a specific focus on quantum gravity theories, I conducted in-depth research on compactifications of extra dimensions in various string theories and explored the applications of AdS/CFT conjecture to condensed matter physics.

For my Ph.D., recognizing the potential of quantum technology, I shifted my focus to quantum physics. Currently, I am investigating many-body systems' equilibrium and out-of-equilibrium dynamics, with a particular emphasis on ultracold atoms in optical lattices. These studies have equipped me with a deep understanding of fundamental problems in many-body physics, as well as valuable experience in quantum optics and quantum information processing.

While pursuing my research, I have also proactively expanded my knowledge and skills in AI and data science. I have successfully completed courses on machine learning and deep learning, where I gained hands-on experience with developing and fine-tuning deep neural networks. I have implemented projects utilizing machine learning techniques and have a strong familiarity with large language models LLMs. Additionally, I have developed my proficiency in SQL for working with big data and am very familiar with pandas libraries, enabling me to handle complex datasets efficiently.

I believe Quantum technology and Artificial Intelligence are the two most prominent technologies of the near future. With my expertise in AI, data science, and quantum physics, I am open to exploring opportunities in AI and/or quantum industries.

If you are seeking a dedicated professional with a strong foundation in theoretical physics, expertise in AI and data science, and a passion for quantum industries, I am confident that my unique skill set and capabilities make me a valuable asset to any potential employer.

I am a theoretical and computational physicist and electronic engineer with a strong passion for AI, data science, and quantum technology. With a diverse educational background, I bring a unique perspective to problem-solving and a solid foundation in mathematics, physics, and engineering principles.
