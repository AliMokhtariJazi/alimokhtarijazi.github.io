Who am I?

I am currently a Ph.D. student and research assistant at Simon Fraser University under the supervision of Professor Malcolm Kennett.

My research interests are pretty broad, covering a range of scientific and intellectual domains. In Physics, my main interests are Quantum Foundations, Quantum Gravity, Quantum Information, and Quantum Many-Body Physics. Artificial Intelligence also fascinates me, particularly Machine Learning and Deep Neural Networks. I am equally captivated by the intersections between Economics and Complex Systems, especially in the context of Many-Body Physics. Last but not least, Philosophy and Logic are topics I would like to study, think about, and discuss with others.

If any of these areas intrigue you as much as they do me, I'd love to connect and explore these topics further. Feel free to get in touch!
